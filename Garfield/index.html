Tale About A Lazy Cat's Life!

This is Garfield

Garfield was born in an Italian restaurant in 1978. At birth Garfield weighed 5 POUNDS & 6 OUNCES!(that is huge for a kitten) Unfortunately the owner of the restaurant was not willing to keep Garfield. (apparently Garfield kept eating all the lasagna). He is a lazy cat who only likes to eat, sleep and watch tv. Garfield was then sold to a pet store. The next thing Garfield knew he was purchased by a skinny cartoonist named Jon Arbuckle.

This is Jon Arbuckle This is Odie

Jon is the owner of Garfield and Odie. He is a geeky and clumsy man. He converses with Garfield and is often the butt of his jokes. Though Jon dislikes what Garfield does but he cannot do without him.

Odie is a dog with a large tongue and slobbers all the time. He can walk and more importantly dance on two legs. Though Garfield frequently calls him a moron but they are good friends.
